Requirements

To Start Your TikTok Shop Journey

To launch your successful TikTok Shop, we'll need a few essential details to ensure smooth setup and optimal performance in this dynamic social commerce platform.

Account & Business Verification

Active TikTok account with business verification eligibility. Valid business registration documents (LLC, Inc., or equivalent) for seller verification. Tax information and banking details for payout setup.

Product & Content Assets

Product catalog with high-quality images and descriptions. Inventory details and shipping capabilities. Brand guidelines and content preferences. Sample products for content creation if applicable.
