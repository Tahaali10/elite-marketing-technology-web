What We Do

Complete eBay Store Management

We manage your entire eBay journey — from account setup and product onboarding to optimization and performance growth. Every step is designed to help you maximize visibility and sales efficiently on eBay.

eBay Account Setup & Store Optimization

  • Seller account creation & verification
  • Storefront design and category structure setup
  • Listing optimization with SEO-driven titles, descriptions, and tags
  • Product photography and enhanced visual content

Product Sourcing & Inventory Management

  • Finding verified suppliers and reliable stock
  • Integration with dropshipping or bulk suppliers
  • Inventory management, stock monitoring, and reorder alerts
  • Order fulfillment, returns handling, and performance tracking

Marketing & Advertising

  • eBay promoted listings and PPC campaigns
  • Category ranking optimization and visibility enhancement
  • Email and remarketing funnels for repeat buyers
  • Feedback, review, and reputation management

Performance Analytics & Optimization

  • Sales performance tracking and analytics
  • Competitor analysis and market positioning
  • Conversion rate optimization strategies
  • Monthly performance reports and growth recommendations

Requirements

To Work Together We Need

To kickstart your eBay Store Management journey, we'll need a few key details to ensure smooth onboarding and optimal performance.

Business & Account Readiness

An active eBay Seller Account or our assistance to create one. Verified business registration (LLC, Inc., or GST Number) if applicable for larger operations.

Product & Inventory Details

Product catalog, preferred categories, or niche. Supplier or distribution authorization documents if reselling brands. Initial inventory plan and budget to forecast growth efficiently.
